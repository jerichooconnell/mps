Apple Bee Festival

Hosted in late September the Metchosin Apple Bee Festival is the communities time to celebrate apples. Hosted at the Metchosin community house. More information is posted closer to the date.

Apple Map

The Apple Map is an effort to catalogue all of the varieties and locations of the apples in Metchosin. If you would like too add your apple trees to the map feel free!
